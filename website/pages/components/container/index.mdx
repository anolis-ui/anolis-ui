## Variants

Container has 2 variants. By default it is **normal**, but you can use also **fluid** which is always 100% width.
Width of normal variant is based on breakpoints. Anolis default theme use this max-widths:

| _ | xxs | xs | sm | md | lg | xl | 2xl
|:-:|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 100% | 100% | 100% | 32em | 40em | 56em | 64em | 84em
